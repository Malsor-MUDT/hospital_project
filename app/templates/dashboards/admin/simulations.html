Simulation Guide

Realistic Parameters

  • • Base Monthly Treatments: Use historical averages or market research
  • • Utilization Rates: Start with historical data, adjust based on device specialization
  • • Seasonality: 0.8-0.9 for slow months, 1.1-1.2 for peak seasons
  • • Maintenance Downtime: Typically 3-8% depending on device age

Statistical Methods

  • • Uses Negative Binomial distribution (better for healthcare than Poisson)
  • • Accounts for over-dispersion in treatment counts
  • • Calculates 95% confidence intervals for estimates
  • • Estimates probability of loss for each device
